./insilico_data_100node_networks

includes the MATLAB files for generating the results presented in the paper, for Figs. 1, 3 and 4.

. Data_results_fig1: folder containing the data and the simulation results for Fig.1 to be included in /insilico_data_100node_networks.

. Data_results_figs_3_4: folder containing the data and the simulation results for Figs. 3 and 4 to be included in /insilico_data_100node_networks.  

. Main_RIVA_fig_1.m: script for generating the results shown in Fig. 1 obtained by RIVA, compared to those of dynGenie3 and Bingo. 

. Main_RIVA_fig_3.m: script for generating the results shown in Fig. 3 obtained by RIVA, compared to those of dynGenie3 and Bingo. 

. Main_RIVA_fig_4.m: script for generating the results shown in Fig. 4 obtained by RIVA, compared to those of dynGenie3 and Bingo.
